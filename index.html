About the Facility

Actual Fitness 1

This private training Suite is for completely private one-on-one training or even small groups. This room is fully equipped with functional training equipment as well as complete selection of all the equipment you'll need for general conditioning and specialized training.

Actual Fitness 2 Under Construction

This 2000 square-foot training room is set up to be used for group training of all types including yoga, Boot Camp, TRX, CrossFit, boxing, karate, MMA, dodgeball, Kettlebells, etc. The right kind of facility for up to 30 participants.

Prison Yard

This very unique facility is designed to be utilized by a group of up to 15 people and their instructor. This is an outdoor training facility using functional training equipment such as tires, sledgehammers, TRX stations and bodyweight exercises all done to the tune of a prison workout.
